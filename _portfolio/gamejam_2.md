      <p>
        A group of trekkers end up trapped in an ice cave. They must use resources to craft parts of a tower to escape. Unbeknownst to the rest, one member has been replaced by 'The Frozen' — an ancient being that wants everyone to remain Under the Ice forever. Requires at least 3 players and a VoIP method for communication.
      </p>
      <p><strong>Gameplay:</strong> One player is randomly assigned 'The Frozen', whose objective is to prevent the others from completing the tower. Victims gather resources and take them to magical generators to create escape tower pieces.</p>
      <p><strong>Controls:</strong></p>
      <ul>
        <li>WASD — move</li>
        <li>Left Shift — sprint (hold)</li>
        <li>LMB — gather resources / attack</li>
        <li>E — interact / pick up</li>
        <li>1 — corruption ability (The Frozen only)</li>
        <li>Esc — menu</li>
      </ul>
