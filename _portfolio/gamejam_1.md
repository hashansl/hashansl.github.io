      <p>
        In a world where only four elephants remain due to rampant poaching, players are entrusted with the vital mission of preventing the extinction of these majestic creatures. Using a magical bow and special arrows, players aim to save falling elephants and accumulate the highest score on the leaderboard.
      </p>
      <p>
        Each elephant saved drops power-ups granting ten special arrows. Special arrows include Burst (3 and 5 arrow variants), Volley (3 and 5 arrow variants), and Homing arrows. The game ends when three elephants hit the ground.
      </p>
      <p><strong>VR Controls:</strong></p>
      <ul>
        <li>Movement — Right Controller thumbstick</li>
        <li>Turn — Left Controller thumbstick left/right</li>
        <li>Menu interaction laser — Left Y / Right B</li>
        <li>Menu interact — Controller Trigger</li>
        <li>Grab/fire — Controller Grab (release to fire; grab bow handle to switch hand, string to draw)</li>
      </ul>
